For state-level policy actions, one of the 50 U.S. states is the entity taking action.

For federal-level policy actions, a US federal agency is the entity taking action (Congress, Department of Defense (DoD), Department of Energy (DoE), Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), Food and Drug Administration (FDA), United States Geological Survey (USGS), Government Accountability Office (GAO), President of the United States, United States District Court) To search for an agency, select it from the drop-down menu or type the full name or acronym of the agency into the Search bar.
