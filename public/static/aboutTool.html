The PFAS Governance Tracker is maintained by the PFAS Project Lab, an interdisciplinary research group focused on social and environmental questions related to per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS). Learn more about the PFAS Project Lab by clicking here. The PFAS Governance Tracker is designed to provide comprehensive and user-friendly information about policy and governance actions on PFAS in the United States at the federal level and in the 50 US States.
